About Sbinc Solutions

Engineering Excellence Since 2008

COMPANY OVERVIEW

Who We Are

Sbinc Solutions is an engineering and services company based in Accra, Ghana, focused on delivering engineering, civil, and electrical solutions. Operating since 2008, we work across both public and private sectors, providing turnkey solutions to complex challenges.


We combine innovative technology with a commitment to continuous improvement to exceed customer expectations.

Our Vision

At SBINC, our aim is to offer turnkey solutions to existing challenges in engineering, electrical and civil works. We strive to ensure that we stay ahead of our competitors to become the leading provider of building, civil engineering and electrical solutions in Ghana and beyond.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing the highest quality products and services in construction, electrical and civil works at very competitive prices. We propose solutions that make use of groundbreaking technology and we continue to work hard daily to ensure that we exceed our customers’ expectations.

Team & Capabilities

Our firm consists of a dedicated team of professionals including:

  • Engineers (Civil, Electrical)
  • Project Supervisors
  • Surveyors
  • Draftsmen
  • Support Staff (Accounts, Management)

We are backed by robust logistics and machinery to fulfill all project requirements efficiently.

OUR NETWORK

Partners & Global Reach

SBINC works as a bridge between international expertise and local engineering needs, acting as a representative and partner for leading global and regional utility agencies.

  • Electricity Company of Ghana (ECG)
