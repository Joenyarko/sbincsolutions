Our Services

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions for Public & Private Sectors

Solar Installation Services

Sbinc Solutions Limited is a complete solar service provider. Leveraging our expertise and skills, we offer our customers efficient, affordable and reliable solutions to their solar needs. We take care of the entire solar process- procurement, installation, service and maintenance in a quest to make the process as stress free as possible for our clients.

Civil Works & Building Construction

Civil Works

We undertake a comprehensive range of civil works which include road construction and maintenance, bridge works, leveling, paving and curbs, sewerage systems, heavy civil infrastructure, etc. Our team of civil engineering experts are driven passionately to find long-lasting solutions to the daily challenges found in civil works.

Building and Construction

We have expanded our portfolio to add building and construction to our repertoire. With advanced project management, we cover small to large-scale construction work to add on real property. Be it commercial buildings or residential construction, our team is able to meet client expectations. These include offices, schools, clinics, shopping malls property development, etc.

Electrical & Telecommunication Engineering

We have gained recognition for our expertise in rendering electrical and telecommunication works to our cherished clients.

From installation of poles, lightning protection systems, and transmission lines to the deployment of fiber optic networks and telecom masts, our team is equipped with the right skills and resources. Our specialization in these fields coupled with our zeal for innovation puts us ahead of the game.

Procurement

We are well connected with diverse suppliers across the world which enables us to deliver a wide range of products in electrical and civil works.

Our comprehensive supplier portfolio and timely delivery services have earned us a hallmark of integrity and reliability in rendering procurement services. We are undoubtedly dependable in delivering quality products to our clients that beat their expectations.

Consulting

Sbinc Solutions has worked as a consultant for the Ministry of Energy on a number of projects including the Self-Help Electrification Project (SHEP) and the Last Mile Electrification scheme.

We have also consulted with private organizations on their projects which cover building and road construction as well as procurement. Our services include efficient project management, risk assessment, and business development.
